## Advanced tips (and how to save credits)

### The 3×3 storyboard trick

Plan your keyframes with a 3×3 grid. One storyboard image plus a short prompt can get you a full video:

1. Draw a rough 3×3 storyboard

1. Each square is a key moment

1. Upload the storyboard and describe what happens in each square

1. The model fills in the transitions

### Scene-by-scene generation (for longer stories)

Generate long videos in chunks so the story stays consistent:

1. Generate the first chunk (for example, 5 seconds)

1. Use the last frame as the next chunk’s first frame

1. Repeat until the whole story is done

### Multimodal combo tips

A practical combo is 3–5 key images + 1–2 reference clips + 1 music track. Consistency can often reach 95%+.
